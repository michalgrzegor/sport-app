Welcome to Sport App

This tutorial will guide you through the basic functions of our tool.

If you want to skip tutorials, all of them are repeatable, so you can go through them later. You can find them by clicking the cog icon.

Program

When you accept the invitation from your trainer, your program will appear on the screen.

Click on a day, and see what your trainer prepared for you.

Questions

Sometimes your trainer will want you to answer some questions.

Click and send him your answers.

Boards

In this tab, your trainer will send you notes.

Notes can relate to your program or be general tips.

Programs

Create your first program. Click the button below and play around with options.

Tiles

In Sport App, to fill a program with activities, first you create tiles.
Tiles represent workouts, meals, questions and notes.

One tile can be used in many programs.

Filling a program

Open tiles collection from left menu, and just drag and drop a tile to your program.

When you open a day in your program, click the floating button and choose plus icon to add a tile.

Client card

In the last step, create your client card.

Use it to connect with your client and share the program you prepared.
Now, he or she will answer your 'question tiles' and see workouts.

Congratulations

Tutorial complete!
